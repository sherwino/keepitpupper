About Us

Here in the puppy lounge, we like to consider ourselves as an open format room you could listen to whatever you feel like listening to. Although we tend to lean towards a few genres, so it is likely that when you come into the room you would hear us playing the following

  • Rap
  • Hip-Hop
  • R&B
  • Deep House
  • Nu-Disco
  • Trap
  • Funk
  • Dubstep
  • Indie
  • Latin
  • Americana
  • well who knows what else, just feel the room out.
    •

The only genre we stay away from is country music. We also like to welcome bluegrass and folk music around these parts.

The Puppy Lounge Does Events:
We don't really do much events, but if we come up with something we will try to let you know.

Monday

On Monday's we tend to play our weekly Discover playlist that Spotify made for us. Usually, this list will consist of songs that we haven't listened to on Spotify, it could be new or old stuff.

Tuesday

Tuesdays are made to be free for all. Go with the flow of the room!

Wednesday

Wednesday's are made to be free for all. Go with the flow of the room!

Thursday

On Thursday's we like to pepper in some throwbacks jams and scour the nets for new releases!

Friday

Fridays are Fresh, this day we try our best to play the newest of the new tunes, with the help of our Release Radar playlists, Fresh Finds playlist, and much more. Jump on and play some fresh tunes!

Sunday

On Sundays, we promote the adoption of homeless pets.

The Puppy Lounge Doesn't Have Many Rules

We pretty much only have FOUR rules:

1. Don't skip, whatever you do just live with your mistakes.

2. Go with the flow, do your best to follow the vibe.

3. If you are following rule 2 you should know that you probably can't throw in any country.
So don't play that.

4. Now, If you play a clean Version of a song you will automatically get a nope
from one of our admins
